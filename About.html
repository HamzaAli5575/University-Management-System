ABOUT US


Air University Islamabad, is one of the top-grossing educational institutes of Pakistan. Air University is under the PAF. University has its name in the top ten best engineering as well as business universities of Pakistan. The department of computing and AI has been considered to be the future research base centre. Our highly qualified faculty have made it possible to publish the most research papers nation wise and to organize the international conference on cyber security and warfare twice. University is located in between the aesthetic view of mountains leading towards our pride the Faisal mosque which makes a drop-dead view and the pleasant environment is yet to be considered.



VISION


Air University aspires to be among the leading national universities, excelling in teaching, learning, research, innovation and public service.



MISSION


The mission of Air University is to achieve excellence in teaching and research for producing graduates with sound professional knowledge, integrity of character, a keen sense of social responsibility and a passion for lifelong learning. The University shall stand committed to creating an environment conducive for attracting the best students, faculty and supporting staff for contributing to the development of a prosperous, peaceful and enlightened society.
