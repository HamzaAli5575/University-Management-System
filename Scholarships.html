Offered Scholarships

AIR UNIVERSITY offers the following schemes to assist the needy cum talented students:-

AU sponsored MS & PhD programs


AU pays back to society through Graduate Research Initiative Program. This program enables the student to take on professional challenges for excellent leadership careers in academia and industry. This scheme offers the following benefits for selected candidates.
A stipend of Rs. 40,000/- per month
Full fee waiver.
Career progression through Lab Engineer, Teaching/ Research Assistant, Post-doc and faculty positions at Air University.

AU need based financial grant

Through this scheme AU funds in each semester to needy students who face the financial constraints ranging from 25 to 50% of tuition fee discount.

AU advance merit scholarship

PAF ward concession and AU employee concession

AU grants 25% tuition fee discount to PAF Wards and 50% discount to AU Employees and their wards.

Ehsaas Undergraduate Scholarship Project (EUSP)

Fazaia Educations Welfare Scheme (FEWS)

Other than 25% Tuition Fee discount PAF Wards studying at AU avail the additional funding from FEWS ranging from 25% to 100%.

Punjab Education Endowment Fund Scheme (PEEF)

This scheme offers AU students to avail 50% of Tuition Fee discount in each semester through PEEF as per the laid down criteria set by the Punjab Govt to sponsor the higher education of deserving students.

PM tuition fee reimbursement scheme

Through this scheme students hailing from the less developed areas of Pakistan (Selected areas) can avail the funding opportunities at Graduate and Postgraduate level.

Educate to Innovate Scholarship Scheme (Only for MS Information Security and MS Avionics Engg)

Tuition Fee Waiver (Full and Partial)

Merit based fee waiver for students with bright academic record. Eligibility criteria is CGPA 3.00 or above in the relevant bachelor degree.

Apprenticeship

Chance to work as apprentice in industry projects after 1st semester for students with bright academic record and relevant skills.
The student will be working on Industry/Research Projects.
Data sharing for aerial vehicles.
GSM Security.
Customized Android with enhanced security features.
Sensor Fusion for mobile nodes.
